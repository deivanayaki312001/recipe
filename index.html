Simple Omlette Recipie

An omelette is a dish made by frying beaten eggs in a pan until fully cooked but still tender. It is a versatile and quick-to-make meal, often enjoyed for breakfast or brunch.

Preparation time
  • Total:Approximately 10mins
  • Preparation:5mins
  • Cooking:5mins

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:grated cheese,cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and beat them with a fork or whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat the Pan:Pour the beaten eggs into the heated pan.Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, pushing the cooked parts toward the center and letting the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.
  3. Add the Fillings:prinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  4. Fold and Serve:Carefully fold the omelette in half, covering the fillings.Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
